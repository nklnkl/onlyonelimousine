ABOUT US

Life can be hectic - we've got you covered.

Based in New York City, Only One Limousine proudly services Long Island and the five boroughs. Your transportation needs are our highest priority, and we cover a wide range of events including weddings, proms, sweet sixteen parties, wine tours, bar-mitzvahs, birthday parties, date nights and more. We'll even give you a lift to and from the airport.

All of our drivers are licensed and certified for Limousine and Chauffer services in accordance with New York State Law and the Department of Transportation.

We offer prompt pick-up and drop-off, and maintain the high-quality service you deserve.

MISSION STATEMENT

At Only One Limousine our goal is simple: to provide our customers with an excellent experience, from pick-up to drop-off.

Let's face it, your leisure time is precious, and whether you're looking to take a wine tour or simply a night out on the town, your transportation service should not get in the way of your enjoyment, it should enhance it.

We maintain the highest standards in professionalism, promptness and safety, but we don't stop there. When you think back to your day or night out, you should do so with a smile - that's our mission.
